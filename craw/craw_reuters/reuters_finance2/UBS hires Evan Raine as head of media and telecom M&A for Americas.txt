    "content": "April 10 (Reuters) - UBS Group (UBSG.S), opens new tab has hired Evan Raine to head its media and telecoms mergers and acquisitions unit in the Americas to help the bank capture more market share from advising on deals in the sector.\nRaine will start at UBS on April 14 and will be based in New York, Jeff Hinton, global co-head of mergers and acquisitions at UBS, according to an internal memo reviewed by Reuters on Thursday.\nA spokesperson for UBS confirmed the contents of the memo.\nRaine joins UBS from Adams Outdoor Advertising, a home advertising operator, where he was Chief Financial Officer for the past two years.\nPrior to that role, Raine was a managing director in the technology, media and telecoms group at Barclays and held several positions across the media and communications M&A group. The bank's previous global head of media and entertainment, Aly Alibhai, left UBS to join Deutsche Bank as its new head of North America M&A last year."
